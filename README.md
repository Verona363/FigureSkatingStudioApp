# FigureSkatingStudioApp
In the application, users can search for and register for individual or group figure skating training sessions.Group training sessions are classified based on whether the training takes place off-ice or on-ice. Off-ice training has two types:
- Stretching
- Jump training (SFP)

Off-ice training sessions do not require level classification. 
On-ice training is classified by skill level: Beginner/Intermediate/Advanced. In addition to the level classification, on-ice training sessions can focus on different skill areas:
- Gliding and spins
- Jump-focused training

Individual training sessions do not have predefined classifications. A user first registers for the session, and then the training focus is discussed with the coach. In summary, off-ice training sessions do not require level distinctions, while on-ice sessions are categorized by skill level.

User Roles
The application has two types of users: Coaches and Clients.

- Both clients and coaches can create an account and log in to the application.
- Only coaches can add, edit, and delete training notifications (training announcements).
- Clients and other coaches can view all training notifications posted in the application.
- Users/clients can search for training sessions using keywords. Clients can also filter training sessions using one or more classifications, for example:
Location (e.g., Pirkkolan jäähalli, Malmin jäähalli)
Skill level (e.g., intermediate)
Training type (e.g., stretching or on-ice training)
- Clients can register for training sessions. Each training notification displays the list of users who have registered.
- Users can search for training announcements based on their scheduled time.
- Clients receive a push notification one day before the training sessions they have registered for.

User Pages
The coach profile page shows:
- The total number of training notifications created by the coach
- A list of those notifications
The client profile page shows:
-The total number of training sessions the client has registered for
-A list of those registrations
-Statistics on the training sessions attended by the client (e.g., number of on-ice and off-ice sessions attended, and the number of group and individual sessions attended per week, month, three months, or year).

The primary data item in the system is the training notification. The secondary data item is the registration.
